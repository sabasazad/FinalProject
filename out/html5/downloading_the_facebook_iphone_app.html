Downloading the Facebook iPhone App

Facebook iPhone App can be downloaded from the Apple AppStore.

1. This task will show you steps to download facebook iPhone App.

2. You need to download the Facebook App to use Facebook on an iPhone.

3. You will download the App to use Facebook on your iPhone to share and connect with people and businesses.

  1. To download the App, Search Facebook on the Apple AppStore.
  2. Tap this (cloud with down arrow) button to begin downloading the App.
  3. When the download is complete, Press the Open button or locate and tap the Facebook icon on the iPhone screen.
You have the Facebook App on your iPhone.
