Creating a Facebook Account

Create a Facebook Account in your Facebook App on your iPhone to use Facebook to share and connect with people and businesses.

Download Facebook app from the AppStore.



1. Use the steps mentioned here to create a new facebook account on the facebook app.

2. Creating a new facebook account is necessary before you can use the facebook app.

3. Facebook app requires you to create a free account in order to use their social media application.

  1. Tap the icon to start the App.
  2. Tap Sign Up for Facebook.
  3. Tap Get Started to Begin.
  4. Enter an email address or phone number.
  5. Enter your name.
  6. Create a password.
  7. Next, you'll need to add some basic information about yourself.
  8. Once you are finished, you'll have a chance to set your profile picture.
  9. You can take a new picture or you can choose from one on your camera roll.
  10. Finally, you'll have a chance to search for people you already know on Facebook so you can quickly begin adding friends. But if you decide to skip this step for now, you'll still be able to search and add friends at a later time.
You have a new facebook account. You can log in with this new account.
